# Email bodies (MailerLite)

Plain-text templates for the `EmailCaptureSlot` list ("ParkMath Alerts"). Verified figures only,
no affiliate links, `{placeholder}` tokens for the dynamic parts. The `content-factory` skill
generates the live weekly digest body from `news.json`; these are the templates + worked examples.

> Hook that wins the signup (already on every page): **"Get notified when {Airport} parking prices
> change."** Nobody else offers it, and the data genuinely changes — so the emails must actually land
> when a fee moves. That's what makes the list valuable.

---

## 1. Welcome / validation email (send immediately on signup)

Proves the tool works the moment someone joins. List the last few verified updates with their
official sources.

**Subject:** `You're on the ParkMath list — here's what's changed lately`

```
Hi {first_name|there},

Thanks for joining ParkMath. We track UK airport drop-off and parking costs against each
airport's own official pages and date-stamp every figure — so you'll hear from us the moment a
price that matters to you actually changes.

A few recent verified updates:

• {news_1_title}
  {news_1_summary}  Source: {news_1_source_label} ({news_1_source_url})

• {news_2_title}
  {news_2_summary}  Source: {news_2_source_label} ({news_2_source_url})

• {news_3_title}
  {news_3_summary}  Source: {news_3_source_label} ({news_3_source_url})

See every UK airport, compared and date-stamped: parkmath.co.uk

— ParkMath
```

*Worked example (figures verified June 2026):*
- *London City introduced a drop-off charge for the first time — £8 from 6 Jan 2026.*
- *Gatwick and Stansted drop-off both £10; Heathrow £7.*
- *Edinburgh and Bristol both rose to £8.50.*

## 2. Price-change alert (the 50%+ open-rate email — send when a fee is verified to change)

Triggered manually each time a `news.json` change card with a `from`/`to` is merged. Short, single
purpose, the before→after is the whole email.

**Subject:** `⚠️ {Airport} drop-off charge just changed`

```
{Airport}'s drop-off charge has changed.

   {change_label}:  {from}  →  {to}
   (verified {verified_at}, per the official {Airport} page)

{one_line_context — e.g. "That's the second rise this year." or "New charge — it was free in 2025."}

{If a free alternative exists:}
You can still drop off free at the {free_alternative_name} ({free_alternative_minutes} min).

Full breakdown + the free options: parkmath.co.uk/drop-off-charges/{airport_slug}

— ParkMath
You're getting this because you asked to be told when {Airport} prices change.
```

*Worked example:*
```
Subject: ⚠️ London City drop-off charge just changed

London City's drop-off charge has changed.

   Drop-off charge:  Free  →  £8
   (verified 2026-06-12, per the official London City page)

New charge — London City had no drop-off fee before 6 Jan 2026.

Full breakdown + the free options: parkmath.co.uk/drop-off-charges/london-city
```

## 3. Weekly digest (Monday — generated by `content-factory`)

Positions ParkMath as the definitive change-tracker in the inbox. Last 5–7 verified items.

**Subject:** `This week's UK airport price changes`

```
The verified UK airport drop-off & parking changes from the past week:

• {item_title} — {item_summary} {if change: ({from} → {to})}
  {source_label}: {source_url}

• {item_title} — {item_summary} {if change: ({from} → {to})}
  {source_label}: {source_url}

(…one short line per item, newest first…)

Every figure is read from the airport's own official page and date-stamped. Compare them all at
parkmath.co.uk

— ParkMath
```

---

### Rules (all three)
- **Verified figures only**, each with its official `sourceUrl` — never an unsourced or rounded number.
- **No affiliate links, no merchant URLs.** Links point only to `parkmath.co.uk` + official sources.
- Plain text; one clear purpose per email. The alert (template 2) is the highest-value — keep it to
  the before→after and the free alternative.
