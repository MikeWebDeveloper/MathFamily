# forum-leads — example

Copy this to `forum-leads.md` (gitignored) and paste your F5Bot alerts / forum thread URLs, one per
line. `forum-scout` drafts one reply per line into `forum-drafts-<date>.md`.

Format — `airport:<slug> | <thread url or quote> | optional note`:

```
airport:heathrow | https://forums.moneysavingexpert.com/discussion/123456 | asking cheapest drop-off
airport:manchester | "what's the cheapest week's parking at MAN?" (r/uktravel) | gate vs pre-book angle
airport:gatwick | https://www.reddit.com/r/uktravel/comments/abc | drop-off £10 complaint thread
```

Slugs are the airport slugs used across the datasets (e.g. `heathrow`, `gatwick`, `manchester`,
`stansted`, `london-city`, `edinburgh`, `bristol`). One lead per line.
